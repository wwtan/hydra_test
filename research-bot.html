--- title: Architecture description: Learn how Research-Bot operates through its intelligent agent architecture ---

Research-Bot operates through a coordinated trio of intelligent agents: planner, researcher, and writer. The planner outlines the scope of the research, creating subtopics and key questions. The researcher collects relevant data based on this framework, using a wide range of information sources. Lastly, the writer compiles the findings into a coherent and well-organized report.

Workflow Summary

  • Create a task-specific agent optimized for the research objective.
  • Break down the core topic into strategic questions that promote balanced exploration.
  • Use a data-collection agent to retrieve information for each sub-question.
  • Summarize the results, maintaining source traceability.
  • Aggregate and refine the content into a detailed, final research report.
